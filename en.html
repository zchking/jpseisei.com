PARTNERS

Strategic Partners

Through close collaboration with world-class international brands, we deliver innovative, high-quality products and solutions to our customers.

Pyxis®

Pyxis is a world leader in inline water quality measurement sensors. They offer high-precision sensors covering a wide range of water quality parameters, including PTSA tracer detection, oxidant measurement, and pH. As an authorized Pyxis distributor, Seisei delivers cutting-edge water quality management solutions to customers worldwide.

Enagic®

Enagic is the pioneer of water electrolysis technology, globally recognized through its "Kangen Water®" brand. Providing medical-grade hydrogen-rich water ionizers, Enagic contributes to health and environmental well-being worldwide. Seisei is committed to spreading the benefits of Enagic products to more people.
